FAQ

All You Need To Know

How do I know if a vehicle listed is genuine?

All vehicles on our platform go through a verification process. We work with trusted dealers and individual sellers, and every listing includes detailed photos, descriptions, and, in many cases, inspection reports so you can buy with confidence.

Can I get financing through your website?

Yes! We partner with reputable financial institutions to offer flexible auto loan options. Our team can guide you through the application process and help you find a plan that suits your budget.

Do you deliver vehicles to other states or cities?

Absolutely. We offer nationwide delivery at competitive rates. Once your purchase is complete, we help arrange safe and timely delivery of your vehicle to your chosen location.

What if the car I buy has a problem after delivery?

We encourage buyers to review inspection reports carefully and ask questions before purchase. Some vehicles may come with warranties or after-sale support. If there's an issue, contact us immediately, and we'll assist in resolving it.

Can I list my car for sale on your website?

Yes! Our platform makes it easy for private sellers and dealers to list vehicles. We'll help promote your listing to attract serious buyers quickly. Contact our team for assistance in creating a high-quality listing.

Is there a fee to use your platform?

Browsing listings is free. There may be small fees for premium services like featured ads, vehicle inspections, or delivery arrangements. We're always transparent about any costs involved.

How long does it take to complete a purchase?

This depends on the seller, payment method, and delivery option you choose. On average, purchases and delivery arrangements are completed within a few days. Our team works hard to make the process fast and smooth.
